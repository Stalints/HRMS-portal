Dashboard Visibility

Employee Dashboards: Published announcements automatically appear on employee dashboards based on visibility settings.

Team members will see announcements relevant to their role and team when they log into the HR Portal.

Notifications

Employee Notifications: Employees receive notifications for new announcements based on their role and visibility settings.

Notifications are sent automatically when an announcement is published. This is UI-only information; no actual notifications are configured in this frontend version.

Security & Access

Role-Based Visibility: Announcements are displayed based on secure role-based access control.

Only authorized HR personnel can create and manage announcements. Secure login and role-based visibility are enforced at the authentication level (UI text only in this version).
