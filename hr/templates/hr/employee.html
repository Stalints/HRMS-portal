Roles & Permissions

  • HR → Full access to HR modules, Employee Management, and settings.
  • Manager → Limited access to employee information and approvals.
  • Employee → Basic access to their own profile, requests, and notifications.

Dashboard Access

Dashboard widgets and navigation are shown based on the assigned role. HR sees full HR analytics, managers see employee-focused metrics, and employees see only personal information and actions.
